C R O A T I A N 

Notes

  1. Courses in Croatian are offered through the Department of Germanic and Slavic Studies.
  2. CROAT and CI courses count towards the new Cultural Identities (CI) Minor; for further information, please ask the Germanic and Slavic Studies undergraduate advisor.
  3. See REES and RUSS course offerings for interest in Slavic languages, linguistics, cultures, history, literature, and film.


CROAT 100s


CROAT 101 LAB,LEC 0.50Course ID: 004344
Elementary Croatian 1
For students with little or no knowledge of Croatian. The basic elements of Croatian grammar with emphasis on oral practice and pronunciation.
[Note: CROAT 101 is not open to students with native, near-native, or similar advanced ability.]
Also offered Online

CROAT 102 LAB,LEC 0.50Course ID: 004346
Elementary Croatian II
A continuation of CROAT 101.
[Note: CROAT 102 is not open to students with native, near-native, or similar advanced ability.]
Prereq: CROAT 101
Also offered Online

CROAT 200s


CROAT 201 LAB,LEC 0.50Course ID: 004347
Intermediate Croatian I
This course is a continuation of Elementary Croatian. It offers extensive practice in both the spoken and written language. Vocabulary building, comprehension, and pronunciation are stressed.
Prereq: CROAT 102

CROAT 202 LAB,LEC 0.50Course ID: 004348
Intermediate Croatian II
A continuation of CROAT 201.
Prereq: CROAT 201
